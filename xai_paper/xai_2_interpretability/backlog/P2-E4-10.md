**Implemented in JULIA** (`lime.jl`, not `.py`) per the Sprint-5 JUTARI contract — reuses the
Phase-B foundation on main (ig_baseline_sweep.jl env layer + scorer + del/ins + oracle |Δy|;

intervene). Every attribution value is a genuine real-ROM re-run.

**Method:** LIME (Ribeiro 2016) over the candidate RAM-cell interpretable space — draw N random
binary present/absent perturbations, mask absent cells to 0 (= the oracle's own occlude!), RE-RUN
the real ROM, weight by the cosine-distance kernel π(z)=exp(-D(z,1)²/σ²), fit a weighted linear
surrogate g(z)=w·z+b (ridge normal equations, LinearAlgebra); coefficients = attribution. Reports
the **surrogate R²** (local faithfulness of g itself) AND the attribution faithfulness vs the
oracle, PLUS the §5 **stability** across seeds.

**Budget (recorded in each §R record):** N=300 perturbations × 5 stability seeds = 1500 real
emulator re-runs per output; keep_prob=0.5; kernel σ=auto(0.75·√#features). Wall: 21:46 for all 6
core games (2 outputs each). Cluster-shardable: `--shard i --nshards n --shard-kind game`,
`--out-dir`, `--roms-dir`/`--where` (runs unchanged under tools/cluster/xai_array_jl.sbatch).
