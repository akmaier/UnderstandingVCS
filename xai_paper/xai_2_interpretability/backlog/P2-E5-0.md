**Results (Pong, f120+30 window; seed 0; commit-stamped):**
- Patching: bit-exact re-run PASS; **recovered == exact for all 7 patches (max|rec−exact| = 0.0)**;

  ball patches move pixels. A-priori target recall = 0.571 — honest finding: paddle_y (RAM[$33])
