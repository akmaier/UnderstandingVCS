## Notes / handoff
experiment_design.md §5 (row "Meaningful/extremal perturbation"). Pixel-level IoU uses T1
(no T3); object-set IoU needs T3.

**Method (jutari):** extremal/meaningful perturbation (Fong & Vedaldi 2017; Fong et al.
2019) optimised by GREEDY forward selection on the TRUE VCS under an AREA budget
(default area = 0.25·C cells; recorded per record). Greedy (not projected-gradient)
because a soft-mask gradient VANISHES on the §1 position/index output — exactly where
the paper wants the method to succeed. The optimised minimal mask = the attribution.
Mask IoU is measured on the do-key (kind,index) so a `ram[i]:set` in the mask matches
the oracle's `ram[i]:occlude` (same physical cell occluded).

**Headlines (content path):** qbert corr 0.94 / IoU 1.0 / p@3 1.0; ms_pacman 0.58 /
1.0 / 1.0; breakout 0.49 / 1.0 / 1.0; pong 0.45 / 0.5 / 0.67; space_invaders 0.49 /
0.33 / 0.33; seaquest IoU 0.0 (HONEST NULL — its oracle movers are all set-mode, which
the occlude-only perturbation can't reach; the harness positive control still passes
corr=1/p@k=1, so the harness is sound and the null is a real finding, not a bug).
Budget = 1912 real-ROM re-runs total over the sweep. Oracle-as-method positive control
