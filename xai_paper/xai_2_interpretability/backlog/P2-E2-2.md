### Result (jutari, Julia, bit-exact; commit-time run)
`verify_labels.jl` runs in the foreground; for each unique candidate RAM byte it boots+replays
to a live frame (inside the Paper-1 conformance horizon), perturbs the byte by small symmetric
deltas (±4, ±8), re-renders the exact framebuffer over horizons {1,2,3,4}, and marks the byte
**verified-causal** iff the perturbation changes the frame. Position bytes whose changed-region
centroid shifts monotonically along an axis get an **offset-correction**: a robust
median-pairwise regression of render-position vs byte recovers the empirical
`render_pos ≈ slope·byte + offset` (the additive constant AtariARI omits / OCAtari hard-codes),
reported alongside the candidate's declared offset.

Per-game verified-rate (unique candidate bytes), each game bit-exact re-run = PASS:

| game | verified | rate | moving objects |
|---|---|---|---|
| pong | 6/8 | 75.0% | 4 |
| breakout | 7/9 | 77.8% | 3 |
| space_invaders | 8/17 | 47.1% | 7 |
| seaquest | 11/23 | 47.8% | 9 |
| ms_pacman | 15/19 | 78.9% | 2 |
| qbert | 6/20 | 30.0% | 1 |
| **total** | **53/96** | **55.2%** | 26 |

The intervention test discriminates (rate strictly in (0,1)): e.g. Pong's AtariARI raw
`enemy_y`/`player_y` (idx 50/51) are **inert** — the CPU overwrites them before they reach the
renderer, so they are scratch/shadow cells, not the live render-driving cell. This is the
present-vs-used gap (Hewitt & Liang) made causal. Artifacts: `out/verified_<game>.{json,npz}`
(SPEC §R, validated JSON+NumPy round-trip). Self-check (`--selfcheck-only`) asserts bit-exactness,
that Pong ball_x/ball_y verify and move objects with recovered offsets, and that the rate
discriminates. Run: `julia --project=<repo>/jutari tools/xai_study/t3/verify_labels.jl`.

Caveat (transparent): the recovered offset is jutari's render-coordinate mapping (the diff-centroid
slope is ~0.5 for a 2px ball footprint since it averages old+new positions), so it intentionally
differs from OCAtari's reference-frame offset; `offset_agrees` records that comparison, and the
regression points are persisted for provenance.
