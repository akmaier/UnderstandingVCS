`saliency_core_summary.json` (13 files).

Self-check PASSED on all 6 core games. Headline (CONTENT path, |∂y/∂u| vs oracle |Δy|):
  pong  corr=0.407 p@3=0.667 | breakout 0.750 p@3=1.0 | space_invaders 0.215 p@3=0.333 |
  seaquest 0.019 p@3=0.667 | ms_pacman 0.811 p@3=1.0 | qbert 0.999 p@3=1.0.
POSITION/INDEX output: the gradient VANISHES on ALL 6 (grad L1=0, corr=0) — the §7 "Fail / zero
on index outputs" prediction, in numbers. Harness positive control (oracle's own |Δy|) scores
corr=1 / precision@k=1 on every non-degenerate column. The honest 'plausible ≠ faithful' story:
saliency = "what y is a function of NOW" (the self-byte) ≠ the oracle's "what CAUSES y over the
horizon" (frequently an upstream byte) — so content-path corr swings from 0.019 (seaquest) to
0.999 (qbert), and is zero on every position output.
