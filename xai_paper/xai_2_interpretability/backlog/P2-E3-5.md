Method = regional pooled-activity ("LFP") power spectra: pool z-scored varying RAM cells
into a GLOBAL region + per-T3-family regions, detrend (remove the frame-counter ramp) +
Hann window, dependency-free O(T²) DFT (no FFTW added to the shared env). Report dominant
periodicities (frames) and the **%-variance attributable to the known clocks** = the
fraction of each pooled signal's variance captured (LS projection) by the known-clock
basis = frame-cadence harmonics (the fundamental T2 clock, ALWAYS present) + sin/cos
harmonics of every structurally-detected free-running counter cell (constant-stride ramps
+ fixed-period timers). DESCRIPTIVE (no oracle F/S/M); positive control (a clock signal
scores ≈1) + negative control (an off-grid mid-band sinusoid scores <0.5) gate the score.

Headline scalar (SPEC §R value) = the GLOBAL pool clock-explained variance fraction
(epiphenomenal): pong 0.689, breakout 0.368, space_invaders 0.476, seaquest 0.690,
ms_pacman 0.717, qbert 0.678. Per-region contrast IS the A5 finding: counter-driven pools
(score/dots/oxygen/enemy) score 0.85–0.99 clock-explained while genuinely game-driven
pools (player paddle/position) score ~0.0–0.4 — the dominant LFP spectral peaks are mostly
the known clocks (the quantified-Kording lesson). qbert has 0 detected RAM counter cells —
a RECORDED finding (frame cadence still anchors the score), not a failure.
