\section{Claims and evidence}\label{supp:claims}

Every headline claim in the paper rests on a specific artifact, and the claims-and-evidence
table makes that dependency checkable. We list each claim, the figure or table that carries
it, the games and methods it draws on, the metric, the artifact it traces to, and the
limitation that bounds it. The faithfulness, sufficiency, and minimality references are the
triad axes of \S3.4, and the numbers themselves are sourced in Sec.~\ref{supp:provenance};
the table below names which evidence supports which claim, not the values.
