What it does

Runs NMF/PCA on the state tensor and matches the latent components to known signals (clock, read/write, vsync).

How it's scored

Fraction of NMF components that match a known signal/variable (vs the oracle's importance). 0.8 — recovers most known factors, adds some mixed ones.

The score is measured against the §1 intervention oracle — never against another interpretability method. F (faithful) is always vs the oracle; see the execution stack.
