What it does

Grad-CAM, attention rollout and VIPER need convolutional/attention layers or a learned policy that the VCS does not have. Recorded honestly as 'does not apply' rather than forced.

How it's scored

Count of audited methods that cannot run on the VCS at all (they need conv/attention layers or a learned policy). 6 — recorded honestly, not forced into a number.

The score is measured against the §1 intervention oracle — never against another interpretability method. F (faithful) is always vs the oracle; see the execution stack.
